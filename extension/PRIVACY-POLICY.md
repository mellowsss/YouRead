# YouRead Extension - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 2024

## Overview

YouRead is a browser extension that helps you track manga reading progress. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### Local Storage Only
- All manga tracking data is stored **locally** in your browser
- Data is stored using Chrome's `localStorage` API
- No data is sent to external servers
- No data is collected by us

### What We Access
- **MangaNato pages**: To detect and extract manga information
- **Your YouRead website**: To sync manga data (only when you click "Add to YouRead")

### Permissions Explained
- `storage`: To save your manga list locally in your browser
- `tabs`: To detect when you visit MangaNato pages
- `activeTab`: To extract manga information from the current page

## Third-Party Services

### MangaNato
- The extension reads public information from MangaNato pages
- We do not send any data to MangaNato
- We only extract manga details (title, cover, chapters, etc.)

### Your YouRead Website
- When you click "Add to YouRead", manga data is sent to YOUR YouRead website
- This data is only sent to the URL you configure in the extension
- We do not have access to this data

## Data Security

- All data remains on your device
- No servers are involved in data storage
- No accounts or login required
- You have full control over your data

## Your Rights

- **Delete Data**: Clear your browser's localStorage to remove all data
- **Export Data**: Your data is stored in browser localStorage (you can access it via browser dev tools)
- **No Tracking**: We do not track your browsing or collect analytics

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through your YouRead website.
